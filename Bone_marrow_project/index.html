Once the doctors have decided that donor and recipient are fit to undergo the surgery and permission has been granted for the same by the competent authority/authorization commitee, a day will be fixed for the live donor transplant operation. the patient and the donor have to get admitted to the hospital at least 48 hours before the operation. It's mandatory that both patient and the donor again undergo tissue or lymphocyte cross match the test to make sure that the kidney recipient has not formed any antibodies can appear at any time even if they were not detected at the of preliminary tests.

Before surgery the donor and the recipient along with their immediate family members will be counseled again regarding the advantages and disadvantages of kidney transplant. One can still decide to undergo the operation or reject at this stage also. the process of counselling and consent for the surgery will be video graphed and documented.

Patient and the family should undestand that kidney transplant surgeryis not 100% successful surgery. Five to ten kidneys may not work and patient has to go back on dialysis immediately. or with in short time due to nonfunctioning of the transplanted kidney. The rejected or nonfunctioning kidney cannot be putback to the donor under usual circumstances.

The donor and the recipient will be operated upon simultaneously by team of doctors. Who should undergo Kidney Transplant?

One has to undergo kidney transplant when the kidney have completely failed and are dependent on dialysis for survival and there is no possibility of recovery from the kidney failure. This starge of kidney failure is called end stage kidney disease or CKD Stage 5 D+.

Kidney transplant is a procedure where in a kidney from suitable healthy living donor or a deceased(cadaver) donor is transplanted to a patient with end stage kidney failure.
